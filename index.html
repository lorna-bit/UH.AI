Our Vision

UHai is a cultural tech lab building a future where African identities are not an afterthought — they are the blueprint. We experiment with digital storytelling, Afro-centered design, and cultural prototyping to shape a future where AI reflects the fullness of Black life.

Why We Exist

Most digital avatar apps and AI systems offer only the surface — skin tone. But Blackness is more than pigment. It's texture, language, rhythm, resistance, and memory. UHai challenges generic representation and offers something deeper: AI personas born from African cultural logic.

Our Work

  • Momi: An AI mother inspired by matrescence — the transformation into motherhood, rooted in African anthropology and tenderness.
  • [Coming Soon] Second AI persona grounded in Afro-futurism, rebellion, and beauty without compromise.
  • Cultural Prototyping: We create and test Afro-centered digital identities using code, narrative, and aesthetics.

What We’re Building

An app (or platform) that offers AI characters with authentic African hair, features, and stories — not just brown skins with Eurocentric styles. Our goal is a Black-led AI ecosystem where representation is real.
